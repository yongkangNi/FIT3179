Covid 19 Visualisation

some visualization to explore the COVID-19 in the worldwide

Choropleth visualisation in the world map

Within the choropleth shows that there is some country have higher death rate in the dark color like Mexico, china, Italy and Canada. There are three countries (italy,Mexico,Yemen) within over 1000 death in per 10,000 confirmed.

in the Bubble plot, it shows the relationship between the total population and confirmed cases in the different continents. The size is using the deaths per 10,000 confirmed. As we known that United states of America is suffering from the COVID-19 with lot of confirmed cases, but the deaths are controlled relatively in a standard amount.

The number of confirmed in each continent

in the bar char, the amount of confirmed in per 10000 population have the highest value which means that in this outbreak of virus that have a huge impact and the slower response in the virus protection in the Asia.

in the Radial Plot, it illustrates that the amount of active, death, confirmed, Recovered in the different country.
